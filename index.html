About Me

My name is Dustin George. I come from a film background specializing in lighting. While lighting was my main passion I still enjoy writing. I am currently working on writing and drawing a graphic novel in my spare time.

I took up coding because I wanted a career change in a field that is constantly growing. Technology has always been an interest of mine. I am excited to see where the next few years will take me in the developer world.

I also enjoy video games. It is a dream of mine to learn woodworking so I may build a gaming cabinet from complete scratch.

I am also really into beer. I have done a few homebrews myself as well as worked in a brewery. It was great. I got to drink on the job. How cool is that shit?
